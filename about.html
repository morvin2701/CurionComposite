PULTRUSION PROCESS

Pultrusion is a continuous composite manufacturing process where glass fibers are resin impregnated, pulled through a heated die, and cured to form constant cross-section profiles.

KEY PROCESS ELEMENTS

  • Continuous glass fiber rovings
  • Polyurethane resin system
  • Precision heated Steel molds
  • Automated pulling & cutting system

RAW MATERIAL SYSTEM

Glass Fiber

Primary load-bearing skeleton; high tensile & flexural strength.

Polyurethane Resin

Toughness, fatigue resistance, thermal stability.

Protective Coating

UV resistance, aesthetics, abrasion protection.

Surface Mat / Felt

Improved transverse strength and surface finish.
