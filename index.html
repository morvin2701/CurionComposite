Performance Reinvented

KEY PERFORMANCE ADVANTAGES

Advanced composite technology built for strength, stability, and efficiency.

Superior Tensile Strength

Offers 4–4.5× higher tensile strength than aluminum, ensuring exceptional durability and structural stability in extreme conditions.

Thermal Expansion Compatibility

Thermal expansion closely matches glass, reducing sealant failure and minimizing the risk of glass cracking under temperature shifts.

Lightweight Construction

Approximately 25% lighter than aluminum and 75% lighter than steel, enabling easier handling and faster installation.

Reduced PID (Electrically Insulating)

Non-conductive material eliminates grounding requirements and prevents leakage currents, ensuring safer operation.

Cost-Efficient System

15–20% overall system savings compared to aluminum frames, making it a highly economical choice for large-scale projects.

Design Flexibility & Precision

Advanced pultrusion technology enables consistent profiles, tight tolerances, and customizable designs.

Versatile Applications

COMPOSITE FRAMES APPLICATION SCENARIOS

The wide application can meet the demands of the market and has its unique advantages in various fields.

SEA AND OFFSHORE

Superior corrosion resistance against salt mist and humid environments. High-strength composite material ensures structural integrity in harsh offshore conditions.

DESERT

Strong load resistance with 100% rebound after load release. Thermal expansion matches glass, preventing deformation and module stress in extreme temperature shifts.

ICE AND SNOW

Exceptional load-bearing capacity withstands heavy snow accumulation. Low thermal conductivity minimizes ice adhesion and maintains energy yield in freezing climates.

BLACK AESTHETICS

Sleek, matte black finish for seamless integration with modern architecture. A premium, non-reflective choice for residential and commercial rooftops.
